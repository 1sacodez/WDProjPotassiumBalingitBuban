About

KKQuiz! is a fun and interactive website to quiz you on how well you know selected students of 9-Potassium Batch 2029! With different sets of multiple-choice questions, and a time limit right in front of you as you answer, the pressure's on as you go about the quiz and test your Knowledge on K-2029! Be prepared, because the questions could be as easy as their age or their favorite color, or as difficult as—!..... Oh, what, you thought we were gonna spill? Haha! Now go ahead! Choose your minion, start the quiz, and see... how much do you really know? Find out, wander about, and conquer, the Potassium(K) Knowledge Quiz!
